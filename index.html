About Steph


Stephanie is currently a student at Code Fellows working on a career change. She has spent many years working in seasonal hospitality, working in several different states. This industry has given her a great deal of perspective in regards to customer service, teamwork, and multi-tasking. She has developed tremendous knowledge and appreciation for well crafted food and bev, and was pursuing a career in wine prior to pursuing coding. She even has a Cicerone beer certification as well as a level two WSET sommelier certification.

Stephanie is a former student of Fine Arts, with a passion for mixed media, oil painting and photography. She has a genuine love of knowledge and learning, and thus seeks out opportunities to truly understand all of her passions. She is a certified yoga instructor and PADI Advanced Open Water certified diver. With her love of the outdoors she has developed many skillsets. She is a skilled backpacker, having completed a thru hike of the Appalachian Trail. She has proficiency snowboarding, packrafting, and bicycle touring (having toured both the Camino de Santiago & a portion of the Camino del Cid in Portugal & Spain). It doesn't matter what the activity is, when she decides she wants to accomplish something, she dedicates her being to it to achieve her goal.

At this current time, her passion is coding. She is devoted to developing her skillset so she can pursue a career in tech developing equitable software solutions that both assist and inspire a diverse group of users.
